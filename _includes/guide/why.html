Understanding the calculations of statistics, and being able to perform statistical analysis may be useful in most experimental branches of science, for example psychology, sociology, computer science, or any branch of natural science. Editing the Statistics volume is a good opportunity to

  1. study how statistical methods work at the mathematical and conceptual level;
  2. practice statistical analysis using the open source program "R";
  3. share guidance on how to use R-markdown to interact with R in accessible ways.
